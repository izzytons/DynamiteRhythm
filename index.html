Dynamite Rhythm has been providing Southern New England with an authentic Rock & Roll Dance Party since 2016. This tight-knit quintet strives to bring new life to the the classic sounds of your favorite 45's!


Loosely named from the Northern Rhode Island exclusive "Dynamite" sandwich, this group entertains audiences of all ages!


Carefully selecting songs spanning 50 years of jukebox gold, Dynamite Rhythm's 4 vocalists all sing lead and harmony on top of a solid rhythm section. Add a sizzling trumpet on top and you've got one "Dynamite" show!
